📋 Summary: We collect only what we need to run the Service. We never sell your data. Your API keys are encrypted. You can delete everything anytime.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use NeuralTrade AI:

Account Information:

  • Name and email address (for account creation)
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — we never see your actual password)
  • Account preferences and settings

Trading Information:

  • Exchange API keys (encrypted with AES-256)
  • Trade history and performance data
  • Bot configuration settings

Usage Information:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Feature usage statistics (anonymous)
How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide and operate the NeuralTrade AI Service
  • Execute automated trades on your behalf via your API keys
  • Send you trade notifications via Telegram
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve and optimize our Service
  • Send important account and service updates

✅ We never use your data for advertising. We never sell your data. We never share your data with third parties except as required by law.

API Key Security

Your exchange API keys are the most sensitive data we handle. Here's how we protect them:

  • AES-256 encryption — keys are encrypted before storage
  • Read+Trade only — we only request permissions needed for trading
  • No withdrawal access — we cannot withdraw your funds under any circumstances
  • Secure transmission — all data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Access logging — all API key usage is logged and auditable
Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your personal information is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • API keys are immediately deleted upon account deletion
  • Trade history may be retained in anonymized form for statistical purposes
Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your trade history as CSV
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at: privacy@neuraltrade.ai

Cookies

We use minimal cookies strictly necessary for the Service to function:

  • Session cookies — to keep you logged in
  • Preference cookies — to remember your settings

We do NOT use advertising or tracking cookies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our website. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@neuraltrade.ai
